# Contributing to AI Behavior Science

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this repository.

We welcome thoughtful additions that help define, test, and extend the field of **AI Behavior Science**.

## How to Contribute

You can contribute by:

- proposing conceptual frameworks or taxonomies;
- suggesting literature, case studies, or benchmark directions;
- improving documentation or structure;
- contributing computational artifacts, analyses, or reproducible evaluations.

## Proposing Additions to the Field

If you want to propose a new concept, framework, or research direction:

1. open an issue with a clear summary;
2. explain the motivation and relevance to AI Behavior Science;
3. include references, assumptions, and expected value to the field.

## Issues and Pull Requests

- Use **issues** for questions, proposals, corrections, or discussion.
- Use **pull requests** for concrete changes to text, structure, or artifacts.
- Keep submissions focused, clearly explained, and appropriately documented.

## Standards for Scientific Rigor

Contributions should aim for:

- conceptual clarity;
- explicit assumptions and scope;
- reproducibility where computational claims are involved;
- careful citation of prior work;
- professional and evidence-based discussion.

## Code of Conduct

By participating in this repository, contributors are expected to follow **GitHub’s Community Code of Conduct** and maintain a respectful, constructive tone in all interactions.
